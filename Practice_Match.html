Practice Sessions and Matches

At CrickSync Academy, we believe that practice makes a man perfect. In order to enable our players to strengthen their foundation, we offer a solid mix of drills and sessions which include:

  • 4 Practice Sessions a Week of 3 Hours Each
  • 2 Match Days
  • Net Practice Sessions for improving in Batting & Bowling
  • On-field drills and sessions for Fielding and Wicket-keeping
  • Physical, Mental Fitness and Motivation Sessions

To ensure our players get maximum opportunities to learn and seek answers for their doubts, our coach to student ratio is maintained at 1:20. One Coach for a batch of 30 students.

What Make Us Different

  1. Certified Coaches: Our panel of coaches comprises of renowned former players and BCB certified coaches
  2. Customized Modules: Our coaching modules are customized as per age and ability
  3. Hands-On Approach: Our approach towards coaching is hands-on and our coaches are available at every step for the players
  4. In-Depth Analysis: We provide an in-depth video analysis to correct and improve the technical aspects of the game
  5. Lifestyle Guidance: Detailed information and guidance are provided about balanced diet and lifestyle
  6. Treatment and Training: Treatment and training by certified physios and trainers
  7. Sessions and Tests: Coaching includes mental conditioning sessions, fitness sessions and fitness tests
  8. International Exposure: International exposure is provided through frequent coaching camps and tournaments
